1. Information We Collect

AstroTrack collects and processes the following categories of information to provide and improve our services:

üåç Location Data - Why We Need It

AstroTrack requires your location to provide accurate astronomical calculations:

  • Satellite Pass Predictions: Calculate when satellites like the ISS will be visible from your exact location
  • Sky Positioning: Show you exactly where to look in the sky for satellites and space objects
  • Local Space Events: Notify you of space events visible from your area
  • Accurate Timing: Provide precise rise and set times for astronomical objects

We DO NOT: Share your location with third parties, use it for advertising, or store it permanently.

Device Information

We collect basic device information including device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information to ensure the App functions properly on your device and for ad serving.

Usage Data and Analytics

We collect information about how you interact with the App through Firebase Analytics, including:

  • Features used and screens viewed
  • Time spent in the App
  • Space events viewed and satellite tracking sessions
  • Technical error logs and crash reports
  • App performance metrics

Advertising Data

To support our free app model, we use Google AdMob and Start.io for advertisements. These services may collect:

  • Device advertising identifiers
  • IP address and general location (for ad targeting)
  • Ad interaction data (clicks, views)
  • Device characteristics for ad optimization

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: Satellite tracking, space event monitoring, and astronomical calculations
  • Location Services: Provide accurate satellite pass predictions and sky positioning specific to your location
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns, identify bugs, and improve performance
  • Ad Support: Display relevant advertisements to keep the app free
  • Space Data Integration: Fetch real-time data from NASA, SpaceX, and other space agencies
  • Technical Support: Diagnose issues and provide customer support

5. Location Permission Disclosure

üìç Why AstroTrack Needs Location Access

When you first use satellite tracking features, AstroTrack will request foreground location permission. Here's exactly why:

  • Satellite Visibility: To calculate which satellites are visible from your specific location
  • ISS Tracking: To predict when the International Space Station will pass over your area
  • Sky Mapping: To show accurate star maps and constellation positions for your location
  • Real-time Calculations: Location is only used when the app is open and active

Important: We do NOT use background location access. Location is only accessed when you're actively using satellite tracking features.
