1. Information We Collect

AstroTrack collects and processes the following categories of information to provide and improve our services:

Location Data

The App requires access to your device's location services to provide satellite tracking functionality. This includes GPS coordinates and other location-related data necessary for calculating satellite passes, ISS tracking, and astronomical observations.

Device Information

We collect basic device information including device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information to ensure the App functions properly on your device.

Usage Data

We collect information about how you interact with the App, including features used, time spent in the App, space events viewed, satellite tracking sessions, and technical error logs to improve user experience and app performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide satellite tracking, space event monitoring, and astronomical data services
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, identify bugs, and improve the App's performance, features, and user experience
  • Technical Support: To diagnose technical issues and provide customer support when needed
  • App Functionality: To ensure the App operates correctly across different devices and Android versions
  • Space Data Integration: To fetch and display real-time data from NASA, SpaceX, and other space agencies

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App (such as Firebase, Google Analytics), subject to confidentiality agreements
  • Space Data APIs: We send API requests to NASA, SpaceX, N2YO, and other space agencies to fetch astronomical data (no personal information is shared)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, legal process, or government request
  • Protection of Rights: To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, where user information may be transferred

4. Third-Party Services

AstroTrack integrates the following third-party services that may collect and process data:

Firebase (Google)

The App uses Firebase for authentication, analytics, cloud storage, and push notifications. Firebase may collect and use data including device identifiers, IP address, and usage information in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Space Data APIs

The App fetches data from NASA APIs, SpaceX APIs, N2YO.com, and European Space Agency APIs. These services may collect basic request information but do not receive personal user data.

Important: For detailed information about how Google collects and processes data, please review the Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.
